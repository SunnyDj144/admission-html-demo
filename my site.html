ABOUT PEC

Prathyusha Engineering College was founded in 2001 by Shri P. Raja Rao, and is located in Tamil Nadu, India.
It offers engineering courses at undergraduate and postgraduate levels.

The college is affiliated with Anna University

OBJECTIVES

  • To train the students based on the industries current requirement.
  • To improve the technical knowledge of the students on current technologies.
  • To enhance business communication knowledge among the students.
  • To strengthen the domain knowledge of students in Web application Development, Mobile Application Development, Data Science, IoT, etc.
  • To make the students utilise the different opportunities available widely.
